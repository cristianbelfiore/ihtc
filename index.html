This page provides supplementary material for the paper and presents an interactive visualization of the Instance Space Analysis (ISA) conducted on the IHTC 2024 benchmark instances.

Instance Space Projection (Section 4.1)

The projection of instances in the (Z1, Z2) space is obtained through dimensionality reduction via PILOT, and highlights the relationship between instance characteristics and algorithmic performance. The separating boundary between easy and hard instances is approximated through logistic regression, allowing a clearer interpretation of instance difficulty across the space.
